About Sri Krishna Institute of Technology

Institution Overview

Sri Krishna Institute of Technology (SKIT) is a premier engineering institute located in Bangalore, India. Located in the picturesque Chimney Hills area in Hesarghatta, the institute is known for its academic excellence and modern infrastructure.

Our Mission

To provide world-class education in engineering and technology, fostering innovation, research, and professional excellence while developing socially responsible citizens.

Why Choose SKIT?

  • Experienced faculty with industry expertise
  • State-of-the-art laboratories and facilities
  • Strong industry connections and placement records
  • Focus on practical learning and research
  • Comprehensive student support services
  • International collaborations and opportunities
